What We Do

Six ways we make you scale-ready.

Every Craftworks engagement is customized, but our work falls into six core practice areas — each one designed to close a specific gap between where you are and where a major retailer needs you to be.

📋
Channel Readiness Assessment

A gap analysis against actual channel requirements. We score your brand across nine dimensions — packaging, pricing, operations, compliance, and more — and show you exactly what needs to happen before a buyer conversation.

📊
Market Intelligence

Category sizing, competitive landscape, and omni-channel analysis. We give you the numbers that proves where your product belongs — and help you choose the right channel partners to approach first.

🗺️
Strategic Roadmap

A 90-day action plan with sequenced milestones, clear ownership, and cost estimates. No vague "next steps" — a real plan you can execute, with the priority work front-loaded so you see momentum fast.

📣
Pitch & Sales Materials

Sell sheets, line sheets, and buyer presentations built to channel-specific standards. We know what buyers look for because we've been in those meetings — and understand both sides of the table.

💰
Financial Modeling & Capital Strategy

Unit economics, margin analysis, funding options, and financial projections. We build the model that answers the buyer's most important question: can we actually make this partnership work?

🌐
E-Commerce & Web Presence

Website structure, DTC copy, and Shopify setup. Interested buyers Google you before they meet you — your web presence is part of your pitch. We make sure it tells the right story.

Our Engagement Process

How a Craftworks engagement works.

We've designed every stage so you always know what's happening, what's next, and what it will cost. No surprises — just steady, visible progress toward your retail goals.

01
Discovery Call

We start with a conversation — no pitch, no obligation. Don learns about your business, your product, and where you want to go. We assess fit honestly and tell you straight whether Craftworks is the right partner for your goals.

No charge · 45–60 minutes
02
Channel Readiness Assessment

A rigorous gap analysis measuring your brand against the requirements of your target retail channels. We score you across nine dimensions and produce a clear written report with specific, prioritized recommendations — not generic advice.

Deliverable: Readiness Assessment Report
03
Strategic Roadmap

A 90-day action plan built around your assessment findings. Sequenced milestones, cost estimates for each workstream, and a clear picture of the path from current state to retail-ready. You'll know exactly what to do, in what order, and what it takes.

Deliverable: 90-Day Strategic Roadmap
04
Build Phase

We execute the roadmap together. Website, pitch materials, financial model, operational setup — whatever your assessment identified as the priority gaps. This is where most consultants hand you a deck and leave. We stay and build with you.

Deliverables: All materials identified in Roadmap
05
Handover & Training

You receive every deliverable, fully documented, plus training on the tools we've set up on your behalf. The goal is your independence — you should be able to update, refine, and operate everything we've built without needing to call us.

Deliverables: Full package + training session
06
Optional Ongoing Retainer

For brands that want continued support as they approach opportunities — buyer meeting prep, negotiation guidance, response to buyer requests. Even attending buyer meetings with you and taking the lead. Available monthly or on a project basis. You choose what level of partnership makes sense.

Optional · Flexible terms

Don Jones
Principal & Founder

A National Account Director since 2011, Don has spent the last 20 years managing large consumer retail accounts across apparel, packaged goods, and consumer durables for both large and small North American brands. That experience is what Craftworks is built on. Don knows exactly what a Walmart buyer needs to see in a pitch deck, what unit economics look like to a major retail category manager, how to manage the intricacies of doing business with an auto OEM, and what operational questions will end a conversation before it gets started. Craftworks was founded to give small and mid-market brands access to the same quality of strategic thinking that large CPG companies take for granted — without the agency overhead, the junior team doing the work, or the 90-page report that sits on a shelf. You get Don's experience, directly, on your business.
